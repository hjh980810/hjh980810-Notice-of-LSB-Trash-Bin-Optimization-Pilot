Announcement

LSB Trash Bin Optimization Pilot

To all LSB occupants,

To create a cleaner and more efficient environment, the Facilities Management (FM) department is launching a **Waste Management Optimization Project**. Our one-week data analysis has identified that a significant number of trash bins within the LSB are underutilized.

What to Expect

As a result, we will be conducting a **one-week pilot program** where some underused personal office bins will be removed and consolidated into central, high-traffic locations in hallways and common areas.

Pilot Program Duration

Monday, July 28, 2025 - Friday, August 1, 2025

For more information on the data analysis and the full project plan, please scan the QR code on the right to view the project infographic.
